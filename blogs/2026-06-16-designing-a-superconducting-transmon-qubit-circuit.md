A qubit is the fundamental unit of information in quantum computing, exploiting superposition and entanglement to increase processing power exponentially. Superconducting circuits are the most mature qubit implementation since they build on existing semiconductor manufacturing techniques, and this post focuses on designing the transmon qubit circuit using SIMULIA 3D EM simulation.
