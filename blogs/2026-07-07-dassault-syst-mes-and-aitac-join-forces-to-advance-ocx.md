Dassault Systèmes and AITAC are strengthening their long-standing collaboration to accelerate model-based design and approval in shipbuilding. Through OCX eXchanger, customers can seamlessly exchange standardized OCX data within the 3DEXPERIENCE platform, improving collaboration, traceability and efficiency from 3D design to classification.
