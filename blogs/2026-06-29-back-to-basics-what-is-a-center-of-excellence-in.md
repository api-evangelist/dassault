A Manufacturing Center of Excellence (COE) standardizes global production processes to drive efficiency across multiple sites. Did you know that using platforms like DELMIA Manufacturing Operations Management (MOM), COEs replace disconnected systems with a unified digital foundation, helping manufacturers overcome skills gaps, scale smart technology and accelerate time to value?
