In pharmaceutical and biotech manufacturing, a single mismeasured drop can quietly ruin an entire batch, yet manual, paper-based recipes still leave too much to human vigilance. This post shows how Recipe Management Engineering, a DELMIA module on the 3DEXPERIENCE platform, freezes recipes as authoritative control documents through standardized authoring, version control and enforced execution that connects R&D to the shop floor and stops costly failures before they start.
